Our Location

Serefe! Bahadurabad

It might surprise some to hear the best East Asian Karachi has to offer originated in Bahadurabad of all places. But that’s just where Serefe! had its humble beginnings. It didn’t take long for one store to explode into the family-owned award-winning restaurant chain we see today. With a de-regionalized take on East Asian cuisine, Serefe takes the notion that one place knows the “right way” to serve delicious food. Before the restaurant’s start,Adam Serefe traveled the country honing his taste and skills. Adam pulled inspiration from all of his travels and created a menu that features the best of all East Asian Cuisine.
