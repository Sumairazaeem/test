Serefe! Tipu Sultan Road

Founded in 2009 at Sindhi Muslim, Serefe has opened a number of locations, but still remains a family-owned business who specializes in East-Asian cuisine, homestyle sides, and fresh bakery items, all served in a family-friendly casual dining environment. Named one of “The City’s Best East-Asian Cuisine Joints” by Southern Living Magazine and rated “The Best Restaurant in Karachi” by Food Magazine, Serefe SM was the thirteenth Serefe location and the first in the area.
